NUK Rest Easy Baby Spoons

Designed to make feeding time smoother and more enjoyable for both your baby and you. These spoons combine comfort, safety, and style to help create a positive mealtime experience for your little one.

Soft Tip for Gentle Feeding

The uniquely shaped soft tip is gentle on your baby's teeth and gums, making it ideal for infants starting solid foods at 6 months and up. It ensures a comfortable feeding experience without causing irritation.

Germ-Free Dining Design

With the spoon tip kept above the table, these spoons are designed to prevent cross-contamination. This makes mealtime safer and cleaner, offering peace of mind for parents who care about hygiene.

Spatula Edge for Easy Wiping

The innovative spatula edge allows for effortless cleaning of your baby's mouth while keeping food away from the table. This feature reduces mess and makes feeding more manageable for both baby and parent.

Bright and Fun Colors

Adorned with bold, colorful designs, these spoons are visually appealing to little ones, helping to make mealtime more interactive and enjoyable. The bright colors also make it easier to spot and clean any spills.

Dishwasher-Safe and BPA-Free

These spoons are both dishwasher-safe and free from BPA, offering a safe, hygienic, and easy-to-clean solution for your baby's daily meals. They're perfect for busy parents who want to keep their kitchen organized and stress-free.

Durable & Safe for Growing Little Ones

With their durable construction and safe materials, NUK Rest Easy Baby Spoons are built to last through the early stages of your baby's feeding journey. Ideal for use in home or on-the-go.

Whether you're at home or out and about, these spoons are a must-have for safe, fun, and stress-free feeding. Choose NUK Rest Easy Baby Spoons for a better feeding experience for your little one.
