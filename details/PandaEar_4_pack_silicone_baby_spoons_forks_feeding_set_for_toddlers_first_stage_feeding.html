PandaEar Silicone Feeding Set - Safe, Fun & Easy for Toddlers

Each spoon and fork is thoughtfully shaped to fit in your baby's tiny hands, ensuring easy gripping and reduced risk of injury. The soft, non-toxic material provides comfort and is safe for daily use, while the shallow design prevents overfeeding and minimizes choking hazards.

The ergonomic handles are rounded for better comfort and are gentle on your child's small mouth, making feeding more enjoyable. These durable baby utensils are also dishwasher safe and perfect for sensory play, keeping your little one engaged and entertained while they learn to self-feed.

Whether you're looking for a feeding set for newborns or something that supports your baby's development as they grow, the PandaEar silicone baby utensils are a great choice for parents who prioritize safety and fun.
